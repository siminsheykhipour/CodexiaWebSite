Built for evidence-backed readiness and accountable decisions.

CodexiaTrust helps SMEs make NIS2 preparation understandable, traceable, and maintainable across IT, compliance, suppliers, and management teams.

Transparent by design

Audit logs, approval history, evidence timestamps, and source references help teams explain how decisions were made.

Human oversight

AI-assisted drafts are treated as drafts. People review, approve, and remain accountable for final decisions.

Practical governance

Workflows emphasize owners, due dates, residual risk acceptance, and management sign-off.

Clear boundaries

CodexiaTrust supports advisory and inspection-readiness workflows. It does not claim formal certification or guaranteed compliance.
